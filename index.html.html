CHELSEA RESIDENCES: WHERE LEGACY LIVES

Chelsea Residences by DAMAC, recently launched at Dubai Maritime City, is a luxury waterfront development born from a unique collaboration between DAMAC Properties and Chelsea Football Club. This branded residential project blends the prestige of Premier League football with DAMAC’s signature elegance, offering residents a lifestyle of sporting excellence and high-end living. Located in the heart of Dubai Maritime City—between Port Rashid and Dubai Dry Docks—the development boasts stunning sea views and swift access to key destinations such as Downtown Dubai and Jumeirah Beach.
